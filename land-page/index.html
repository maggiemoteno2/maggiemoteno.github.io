About Pick n Pay

Founded in 1967 as a family controlled business with four small stores in the Western Cape, Pick n Pay listed on the JSE the following year. ... Raymond Ackerman purchased the small chain of supermarkets from Goldin, and went on to develop Pick n Pay into South Africa's largest supermarket group.In 2007 Pick N Pay had a rebrand, as the new Pick N Pay logo which looked different from the original, however certain features of the logo have been retained. The rebranding can be attributed to the change that the business needed in order to bring more energy into the business. Moreover the Pick N Pay group consists of the following brand extensions:

  • Clothing
  • Pharmacy
  • Liquor
  • Organic Product Range
  • Home Shopping
  • Recycling Point
  • Groceries
  • Florist
